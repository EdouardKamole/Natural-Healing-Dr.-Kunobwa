Meet Dr. Kunobwa Godfrey Muwanga

Natural healer dedicated to bringing hope and health across Africa

A Natural Healer. A Missionary. An Evangelist.

Dr. Kunobwa Godfrey Muwanga has dedicated over 13 years of his life to healing through natural remedies. As a certified natural healer and devoted Seventh-day Adventist, he has combined his passion for health with his faith to bring healing to thousands across six African nations.

His journey began in Uganda, where he discovered the power of natural remedies to heal even the most challenging conditions. Since then, he has expanded his mission to Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Ethiopia, and South Africa, bringing hope and health to communities with limited access to healthcare.

Dr. Kunobwa specializes in treating cancer, diabetes, high blood pressure, fibroids, and various other illnesses using 100% natural methods. His approach integrates spiritual well-being with physical healing, believing that true health encompasses body, mind, and spirit.
