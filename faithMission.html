The Evangelist's Calling

Dr. Kunobwa Godfrey Muwanga serves as both a natural healer and an evangelist in the Seventh-day Adventist Church, combining spiritual ministry with physical healing.

Dr. Kunobwa sharing the message of health and healing

Called to Serve

For over 13 years, Dr. Kunobwa has been answering a divine calling to heal both body and soul across Africa. His work as an evangelist in the Seventh-day Adventist Church has allowed him to reach thousands with messages of hope, faith, and natural healing.

The Seventh-day Adventist Church has a long tradition of health ministry, seeing the body as the temple of God and advocating for natural remedies and preventive care. Dr. Kunobwa builds on this foundation, taking healing directly to communities across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Ethiopia, and South Africa.

"He sent His word and healed them, and delivered them from their destructions."

Psalm 107:20

Faith & Healing: Inseparable Partners
